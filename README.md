## Peak Interpolation Formula

$$
\begin{gather*}
f_{peak} \approx f_2 + (\frac{m_1-m_3}{m_3 - 2m_2 + m_1} \cdot \frac{\Delta f }{2})
\end{gather*}
$$

## Doppler Shift Formula

$$
\begin{gather*}
\Delta f = \frac{V_{object}}{c} \cdot f_0
\end{gather*}
$$
